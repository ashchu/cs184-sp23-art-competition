B

Caption

A waving lego figure :D

How is the artwork created?

I used blender to add a cube, then used extrude, scale, move, rotate and subdivide to extend and edit parts of the block. I made the two arms as separate cubes initially and union-ed them with the body afterwards. Then I ran our loop subdivison algorithm on the .dae file to generate the final result.

How long did it take?

The useful steps together probably took 3 hours but it took a long time (~8 hours total) to debug unexpected blender errors
